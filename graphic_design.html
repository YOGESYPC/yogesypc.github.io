JAVA CAFE POSTER

I have used some picture or assests from other sources which are men drinking coffee image that can view inside the laptop screen, cup of the coffee and coding image lines that appear in laptop screen. I did the coffee container typography where the coffee container has been made using the geometry shape and use the envelope distort to visible the text on top of the coffee container. I insert the image in the 3D laptop using the create outlines and then choose either perspective distort or free distort to insert and adjust the coding image together with men drinking coffee image until fit the laptop screen size. I use the geometry shape where I choose the rectangular shape to fill blue color background and I draw circle for gradient color slider. Then, I will edit the position of light where I adjust the size of circle to produce as spotlight.

PARIS NEWSPAPER MASTHEAD FONT STYLE

I used the Adobe Illustrator and use the Chomsky text font to make this Paris word. Then I convert it to outline stroke where I set the offset path. I will swap the Fill and Stroke to make the stroke thinner. Once I have developed the Paris word, I will create the horizontal line to add it inside the Paris word. I will use the Line Segment tool and draw two line. I will blend these two lines to fill up the spaces between these two lines and convert it in compound path. Later I will intersect the most inner text (Paris) and horizontal line.These are below items are used for developing this project design..
